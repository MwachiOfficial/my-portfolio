About Me

I am Matthew Mwachi, a full-stack developer committed to creating seamless, user-centric web applications. With proficiency in React, Django, and Flask, I excel at developing software that combines high performance with an attractive, intuitive interface. Whether it's designing a visually engaging frontend or crafting a robust backend, I prioritize writing clean, scalable code. My passion lies in blending technical expertise with creative design to deliver solutions that make a tangible difference.

Services

UI / UX

I bring together a strong technical foundation with a creative approach to web design, crafting websites that are both visually compelling and highly functional. By focusing on user-centric design principles, I create intuitive and seamless experiences that not only capture users’ attention but also drive meaningful business outcomes. My goal is to build digital experiences that are engaging, effective, and aligned with brand objectives.

Frontend Development

With a deep understanding of React, JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3, I build responsive and interactive interfaces that not only captivate users but also deliver flawless performance across all devices. My focus is on creating smooth, engaging experiences that adapt seamlessly to various screen sizes, ensuring both functionality and aesthetic appeal. By emphasizing efficient code and best practices, I optimize the user journey to enhance satisfaction and drive consistent engagement.

Backend Development

Specializing in Python frameworks such as Django and Flask, I develop secure, scalable backends that drive powerful applications and optimize workflows for enhanced efficiency. By focusing on clean architecture and robust functionality, I ensure backend systems are not only reliable but also flexible enough to support future growth and evolving business needs.

Projects

DeezBUUKS

DeezBUUKS is an online platform where book lovers can explore a wide selection of titles, access detailed information, and check out ratings and reviews. Whether you're searching for your next favorite read or curious about a specific book’s reception, DeezBUUKS offers an easy-to-use interface to discover new books, see ratings from fellow readers, and share your own insights. Ideal for bibliophiles looking to connect with a community that shares their passion for literature!

Review Project

LinkUp

LinkUp is an innovative application created to streamline management of project referrals and the commissions associated. This enables users to effortlessly track the projects that have been referred to them and also projects that they have referred to others.With LinkUp there is simplicity in commission management, ensuring that users can easily monitor their earnings from referrals and maintain transparent,organized records of all transactions, making LinkUp an essential tool for professionals who rely on referrals to grow their business.

Review Project

Teen Space

Teen Space full stack application that is built with React and Flask allows teenagers. Users can login to their accounts and access resources using the React interface, and the Flask backend guarantees a dependable and scalable architecture for handling user data and content. Along with event planning, resource sharing, and a content management system, the app has a strong authentication and permission mechanism to protect the security and safety of its users.
